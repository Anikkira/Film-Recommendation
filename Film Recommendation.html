Film Recommendation

LOVE LETTER

1. Brief Introduction

1.1 Film Title: Love Letter

1.2 Directed by: Shunji Iwai

1.3 Plot:

Hiroko Watanabe, living in Kobe, loses her fiance, Itsuki Fujii, in a mountain climbing accident.

Two years later, on the day of Itsuki's memorial ceremony, Hiroko looks through his high school yearbook and finds an address under his name.

She writes a letter to him and receives a reply from a woman named Itsuki Fujii, who bears a striking resemblance to Hiroko.

The movie alternates between Hiroko and Female Itsuki as they exchange letters.

1.4 Language: Japanese

1.5 Release dates

March 25, 1995 (Japan)

June 12, 1998 (U.S.)
